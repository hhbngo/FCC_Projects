RULES OF THE GAME

  • The game has 2 players each taking turns.
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his potential round points.
  • However, If a player ever rolls a 1, all of their potential round points are lost and their turn ends.
  • The player can choose to hold their round points by pressing the 'Hold' button --All of their round points gets added to their total score-- This ends their turn.
  • The first player to reach the set winning amount of points wins the game.

EXTRA RULES

  • By pressing the cog you will find two addition conditions that you can apply to the game.
  • 1. Rolling two 6's will double the player's current total score. This does not end their turn.
  • 2. Rolling two 1's will remove ALL of the current player's score and ends their turn.
